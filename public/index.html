About Me

I am a highly motivated visionary with an ever-growing enthusiasm for software engineering. As a developer with strong foundational knowledge in front-end/back-end app development, UX/UI, and strategic project planning , I am excited to gain more exposure to the collaborative work environment.

Currently, I study computer science at the University of California: Los Angeles. Determined to expand my skill set, I am always looking for opportunities to grow both as a student and an engineer. I regularly attend local hackathons and workshops hosted by UCLA's ACM. I am also a member of UCLA's IEEE (previously a part of the Open Project Space Program, soon to be a part of the AirCopter Project Program).

In addition, I am currently working under Juni Learning as a private tutor for students ranging between 11 and 18 years old. I have a wonderful time passing on my knowledge of computer science and programming practices to the next generation of developers and problem solvers.

Capstone project for the Open Project Space program at UCLA's IEEE . Designed PCBs, circuitry, and implemented code to create a "mouse" that can self-navigate a maze.
