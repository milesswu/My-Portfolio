About Me

I am a highly motivated visionary with an ever-growing enthusiasm for software engineering, as well as a strong foundational knowledge in front-end/back-end app development, UX/UI, and strategic project planning. Equipped with a passion for peer collaboration, I am excited to work with others and grow both as a leader and team member.

Currently, I study computer science at the University of California: Los Angeles. Determined to expand my skill set, I am always looking for opportunities to grow as a student and as an engineer. I regularly attend local hackathons and workshops hosted by UCLA's ACM and am also a member of UCLA's IEEE (previously a part of the Open Project Space Program, soon to be a part of the AirCopter Project Program).

In addition, I am currently working under Juni Learning as a private tutor for students ranging between 11 and 18 years old. I have a wonderful time passing on my knowledge of computer science and programming practices to the next generation of developers and problem solvers.
